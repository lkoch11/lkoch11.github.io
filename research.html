RESEARCH


My research can be broadly split into three different themes.

Regularity in the vectorial Calculus of Variations:

  • On the Lavrentiev gap for convex, vectorial integral functionals
    With M. Ruf and M. Schäffner. Journal for Functional Analysis, 288, no.1 (2025).
  • Quantified Legendreness and the Regularity of Minima.
    With J. Kristensen and C. de Filippis. Archive for Rational Mechanics, 248 (2024).
  • A note on improved differentiability for the Banach-space valued Finsler g-Laplacian
    With M. Goering Comptes Rendus. Mathématique, 361 (2023).
  • Boundary regularity results for minimisers of convex functionals with (p,q)-growth
    With C. Irving. Advances in Nonlinear Analysis, 12, no.1 (2023).
  • Global higher integrability for minimisers of convex obstacle problems with (p,q)-growth
    Calculus of Variations and Partial Differential Equations, 61 (2022).
  • Global higher integrability for minimisers of convex functionals with (p,q)-growth
    Calculus of Variations and Partial Differential Equations, 60, no. 63 (2021).
  • Boundary regularity for manifold constrained p(x)-harmonic maps.
    With I. Chlebicka and C. de Filippis. Journal of the London Mathematical Society, 60, no. 5(2021).
  • On global absence of Lavrentiev gap for functionals with (p,q)-growth.
  • On the validity of the Euler-Lagrange system without growth assumptions.
    With J. Kristensen (2022)

Optimal transportation:

  • Geometric linearisation for optimal transport with strongly p-convex cost
    Calculus of Variations and Partial Differential Equations, 63, no. 87(2024).
  • Partial regularity for optimal transport with p-cost away from fixed points
    With M. Goldman (2024)
  • Lecture notes on the harmonic approximation to quadratic optimal transport
    With F. Otto (2023)

The Jacobian equation and other nonlinear PDEs:

  • Nonlinear open mapping principles, with applications to the Jacobian eq. and other scale-invariant PDEs.
    With A. Guerra and S. Lindberg, (2020). Advances in Mathematics, 415, 108869. (2023)
  • The Dirichlet problem for the Jacobian equation in critical and supercritical Sobolev spaces.
    With A. Guerra and S. Lindberg. Calculus of Variations and Partial Differential Equations, 60, no. 55 (2021).
  • Energy minimisers with prescribed Jacobian.
    With A. Guerra and S. Lindberg Archive for Rational Mechanics, 242 (2021).

Other areas:

    Nonlinear Differential Equations and Applications 29(75) (2022).
